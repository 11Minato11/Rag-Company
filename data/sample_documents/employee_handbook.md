## 1. Introduction

Welcome to TechCorp Solutions! This handbook serves as your guide to our company policies, procedures, and benefits. We're excited to have you on our team.

## 2. Employment Policies

### Employment Classifications
- **Full-time:** 40 hours per week
- **Part-time:** Less than 30 hours per week
- **Contract:** Project-based with defined end dates

### Probationary Period
All new employees serve a 90-day probationary period during which performance is evaluated.

### Working Hours
Standard business hours are Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Core hours (when all employees must be available) are 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM.

## 3. Benefits

### Health Insurance
We offer comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance plans. Coverage begins on the first day of the month following your start date.

### 401(k) Retirement Plan
- Company match: 100% up to 4% of salary
- Vesting schedule: 3-year graded vesting

### Paid Time Off (PTO)
- 0-2 years: 15 days per year
- 3-5 years: 20 days per year
- 5+ years: 25 days per year

### Additional Benefits
- Flexible spending accounts (FSA)
- Life insurance (2x annual salary)
- Employee assistance program (EAP)
- Professional development stipend ($2,000/year)

## 4. Time Off

### Requesting Time Off
Submit PTO requests at least 2 weeks in advance through the HR portal. Emergency requests will be handled on a case-by-case basis.

### Holidays (10 days per year)
- New Year's Day
- Memorial Day
- Independence Day
- Labor Day
- Thanksgiving (2 days)
- Christmas (2 days)
- Floating holidays (2 days)

### Sick Leave
Employees accrue 8 sick days per year. Unused sick days carry over up to a maximum of 40 days.

### Parental Leave
- Birth parent: 12 weeks paid leave
- Non-birth parent: 6 weeks paid leave
- Adoption: 6 weeks paid leave

## 5. Workplace Conduct

### Code of Conduct
We expect all employees to:
- Treat colleagues with respect and professionalism
- Maintain confidentiality of company and client information
- Avoid conflicts of interest
- Comply with all laws and regulations

### Dress Code
Business casual is our standard. Client meetings may require business professional attire.

### Technology Use
Company-provided technology is for business purposes. Limited personal use is permitted.

### Anti-Harassment Policy
TechCorp has zero tolerance for harassment or discrimination of any kind. Report concerns to HR immediately.

## 6. Remote Work Policy

### Eligibility
Most positions are eligible for hybrid remote work after 90 days of employment.

### Remote Work Schedule
- Standard: 3 days in office, 2 days remote
- Must be in office for meetings and collaborative sessions
- Core hours (10 AM - 3 PM) apply regardless of location

### Equipment
Company provides:
- Laptop and monitor
- Keyboard, mouse, and webcam
- Monthly internet stipend ($50)

### Security Requirements
- Use company VPN at all times
- Lock screens when away from device
- Store all work files on company-approved cloud storage
- Report security incidents immediately

### Communication
- Check email and Slack daily
- Respond to messages within 4 business hours
- Attend video calls with camera on when requested
- Update calendar with availability
